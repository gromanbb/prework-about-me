Maná Fan Site!


Bio

Maná is a Mexican pop rock band from Guadalajara, Jalisco. This band was formed in 1986. Maná is considered the most successful Latin American band of all time, with over 40 million albums sold worldwide.

Official Website: Mana.com


Members

The group's current line-up consists of:

1. Fher Olvera - vocalist/guitarist
2. Alex González - drummer
3. Sergio Vallín - guitarist
4. Juan Calleros - bassist
